# Privacy Policy for VRL Mountain Driver 3D

Effective date: June 25, 2026

VRL Mountain Driver 3D is an offline single-player game. Version 1.0 does not
require an account and does not collect, transmit, sell or share personal data.

The game does not use advertising, analytics, location, camera, microphone,
contacts or external account login in version 1.0.

Game progress and settings may be stored locally on the user's device and are
not transmitted to the developer.

If a future release adds advertising, analytics or online services, this
privacy policy and the Google Play Data Safety declaration will be updated
before that release is distributed.

The developer must add a public support email and hosted privacy-policy URL
before Play Store submission.
