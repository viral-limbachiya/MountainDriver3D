Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in the mobile game Mountain Driver 3D (the "App"). By downloading and playing the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal identity information (such as your name, email address, address, or phone number) directly. However, to help us improve the game experience, we utilize third-party services that collect automatic diagnostic and usage data.

Third-Party Analytics (Google Firebase)

The App integrates the Google Firebase Analytics SDK. Firebase automatically collects certain device and usage parameters, which may include:

  • Device Identifiers: Anonymous mobile device advertising IDs (like Google Advertising ID) and unique installation tokens.
  • Interaction Data: Inside-game progress, level completions, upgrades purchased, and details of item collection.
  • Performance Data: Game launch events, framerate drops, crashes, and internal diagnostics.

2. How We Use the Data

The information collected through third-party services is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To monitor, analyze, and optimize game performance and stability.
  • To understand how players interact with the game in order to release improvements and balances.
  • To resolve technical errors, crashes, and bugs.
