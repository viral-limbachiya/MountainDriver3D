Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 11, 2026

VRL PRO DIGITAL operates the VRL Mountain Driver 3D mobile application (the "Game"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you play our Game and the choices you have associated with that data.

1. Information Collection and Use

We do not collect or request any personal identifiers (like your real name, email address, or phone number) directly. However, the Game integrates third-party services that may automatically collect data to enable operational features, authentication, and game analytics.

2. Third-Party Services and SDKs

To provide high-quality mobile game operations, we use external SDK components. These third-party services maintain their own privacy policies governing their collection and usage of device identifiers:

  • Google Play Games Services: Used to enable secure player profiles, game logins, leaderboard score submissions, achievements, and buddy comparisons.
  • Google Play In-App Billing: Processes payments and manages entitlement configurations for premium upgrades securely through Google Play.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM): Delivers local and cloud-based push notifications directly to your device.
  • AdMob Advertising network: Displays customized mobile ads. AdMob uses device advertising IDs to deliver relevant ads and limit ad repetitive impressions.

3. Local Device Data

Game variables (such as cumulative coins, upgraded suspension level, completed runs, and daily login timestamps) are saved locally on your device within secure configurations and synced optionally using Google Play Games cloud snapshot storage.

4. Data Security

The security of your gameplay data is important to us. Transactions and Google services connections are handled through Google's official native security bridges. No personal user identification is shared or sold under any circumstances.

5. Children's Privacy

Our Game is designed to comply with Google Play's family guidelines. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. Advertising profiles are limited in children's categories in compliance with COPPA/GDPR regulations.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions regarding this Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at the email listed on our Google Play Console developer page.
